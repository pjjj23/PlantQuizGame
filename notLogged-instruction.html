How to Play Flora Quiz

1. Create an Account

Sign up for a free account to start playing and tracking your progress.

2. Choose a Category

Select from a variety of plant-related categories to test your knowledge.

3. Answer Questions

Read each question carefully and select the best answer from the options provided.

4. Beat the Clock

Answer as many questions as you can within the time limit to earn more points.

5. Earn Points and Climb the Leaderboard

Accumulate points for correct answers and compete with other players for the top spot.
